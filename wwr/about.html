Pacific Rapids

Our mission is to create safe, exciting, and memorable white water rafting adventures. Pacific Rapids brings families, friends, and outdoor explorers together while encouraging teamwork, confidence, and respect for nature.

History

Pacific Rapids began as a small family rafting company with a passion for adventure and the outdoors. What started with a few rafts and a small group of guides grew into a company dedicated to helping visitors experience the beauty and excitement of the river. Today, we continue to provide guided trips for beginners, families, and experienced rafters.
